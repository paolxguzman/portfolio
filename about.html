About Paola Guzmán

Paola Guzmán is a production sound mixer based in Los Angeles. Originally from El Salvador, she moved to the United States in 2018 and began studying film as a career in 2022.

She discovered her passion for sound in the spring of 2023, quickly immersing herself in production audio and developing a keen skill for capturing immersive, high-quality sound for narrative, commercial, and independent projects.

She is a participant in the BAFTA Vance Byrd Mentorship Program and currently serves as Vice President of the LAVC Cinema Club, consistently seeking to refine her craft and mentor other emerging filmmakers.

Paola often says that “sound saved her life”: during a critical hospitalization that lasted a month, completing her sound class assignments was what kept her going. This experience sparked a profound passion for the sound profession, which continues to inform and inspire her work today.
