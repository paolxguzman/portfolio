Dear Hollywood

LAVC Anti-Racism Project Short Film

Production Date: October 2024

Job: Sound Mixer, Boom Operator

Dear Hollywood is a compelling short film created as part of the Los Angeles Valley College (LAVC) Media Arts Anti-Racism Film Project. This initiative encourages students to craft short films addressing themes of anti-racism and social justice. The film critiques the systemic racism and lack of representation in the film industry, challenging Hollywood's ongoing patterns of racial stereotyping and exclusion. It calls for more authentic and diverse storytelling, aiming to inspire change within the entertainment industry.
